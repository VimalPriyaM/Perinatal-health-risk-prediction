Welcome to Perinatal Health Risk Prediction

Perinatal health risk prediction is a machine learning model that predicts the risk level for pregnant women based on various health factors.

Our model takes into account factors such as age, blood pressure, blood sugar, body temperature, and heart rate to determine the risk level.

By providing accurate risk assessment, healthcare providers can make informed decisions and provide appropriate care to pregnant women.

About the Project

This project aims to develop a predictive model using machine learning techniques to assess the risk of perinatal health complications.

The model is trained on a dataset containing medical records of pregnant women and their associated outcomes.

It utilizes an ensemble of machine learning algorithms, including decision trees and random forests, to provide accurate risk predictions.
